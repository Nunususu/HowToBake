How to make the cookies

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 whole egg & 1 egg yok
  • 3/4 (6 1/8 cups) cups melted butter
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Step 1

Preheat oven to 165 celsius and line a sheet with parchment paper.

Step 2

Sift together the dry ingredients, the flour, baking soda and salt,mix and set aside.

Step 3

In a large bowl put in the wet ingredients, the brown sugar, white sugar, and vanilla, mix, next melt the butter and pour it in with the sugar, put in the eggs and mix.

Step 4

Pour the flour mixtrue into the wet ingredients pice at a time, fully mix in before pouring in more. You should only have to mix and pour about 3-4 times.

Step 5

Once fully mixed pour in chocolate chips then bake for 15-17 mins.
